# Accessing the Platform

The Laplace Platform runs entirely in your web browser — there is nothing to install. Your administrator will set up your account and send you an invite.

## Requirements

To use the Laplace Platform, you need:

- A modern web browser (Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Firefox, or Safari)
- An internet connection
- An invitation email from your organization's administrator

That's it. No software installation is required.

## Getting access

<Steps>
<Step title="Receive your invitation">
Your administrator will create your account and send an invitation to your email address. The email contains a link to set up your password.
</Step>
<Step title="Set your password">
Click the link in the invitation email. You will be taken to a page where you can set your password (minimum 8 characters). After setting it, your account is activated.
</Step>
<Step title="Log in">
Navigate to your organization's Laplace Platform URL in your browser and sign in with your email and password.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
If you did not receive your invitation email, check your spam folder. If it is still not there, ask your administrator to resend the invite from the Settings page.
</Callout>

## Supported browsers

The platform works best on the latest versions of:

| Browser | Supported |
|---------|-----------|
| Google Chrome | Yes (recommended) |
| Microsoft Edge | Yes |
| Mozilla Firefox | Yes |
| Safari | Yes |

<Callout type="tip">
For the best experience, we recommend using Google Chrome on a desktop or laptop computer. The Collector module is also optimized for mobile browsers (Chrome on Android, Safari on iPhone).
</Callout>

## Need help?

If you have trouble accessing the platform:

- **Forgot your password?** Contact your administrator — they can resend your invitation link, which lets you set a new password.
- **Account deactivated?** Contact your administrator to reactivate your account.
- **Cannot reach the platform?** Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, contact your administrator.
