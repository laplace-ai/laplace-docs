# Login

The login page is the entry point to the Laplace Platform. It authenticates users with email and password credentials and takes you to your default starting page.

{/* TODO: Add screenshot of the login page */}

## Login page

The login page features a clean, centered card with:

- **Laplace logo and name** at the top
- **Language toggle** (top-right corner) to switch between PT-BR and EN-US before logging in
- **Email field** with placeholder "you@company.com"
- **Password field** with masked input
- **Sign In button** with loading state

The page uses the platform's design system — warm neutrals in light mode, neutral grays in dark mode — and adapts to your system theme preference.

## How to log in

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter credentials">
Type your email address and password. Both fields are required. Your browser can autofill saved credentials.
</Step>
<Step title="Submit">
Click **Sign In** (or "Entrar" in Portuguese). The button shows a loading spinner while the request is in progress.
</Step>
<Step title="Access the platform">
After successful authentication, you are redirected to the platform's main page — the Digital Twin map by default.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Error handling

If authentication fails, an error message appears below the sign-in button. Common error scenarios:

| Error | Cause | What to do |
|-------|-------|------------|
| "Failed to sign in" | Incorrect email or password | Double-check your credentials and try again |
| Network error | Service is unreachable | Check your internet connection or contact your administrator |
| Account inactive | Your account has been deactivated | Contact your organization's administrator |

<Callout type="info">
There is no "forgot password" self-service flow. If you forget your password, contact your organization's administrator. They can resend your invite link, which lets you set a new password.
</Callout>

## Session management

After login, the platform manages your session automatically:

- Your session is maintained in the background — you do not need to log in again during normal use
- If your session expires after extended inactivity, you will be redirected to the login page
- Session refresh happens silently — you will not see any interruption during normal use

## Signing out

To sign out:

1. Click your **user avatar** at the bottom of the sidebar
2. In the dropdown menu, click **Sign Out** (or "Sair" in Portuguese)

This clears your session and redirects you to the login page.

## Language on the login page

The login page includes a language toggle that lets you switch between Portuguese and English before signing in. This is useful for:

- New users who prefer a different language from the browser default
- Shared devices where different operators may prefer different languages

The language preference is preserved after login and applies to the entire platform.
