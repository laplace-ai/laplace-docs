# Welcome to Laplace

Laplace is a **digital twin platform for logistics networks**. It gives logistics operators and carriers a real-time, intelligent view of their entire operation — from individual shipments to network-wide patterns.

The platform combines live data, predictions, and interactive visualizations to support better decisions at every level of logistics operations.

## What is a digital twin?

A digital twin is a virtual replica of a physical system. In the context of logistics, the Laplace digital twin mirrors your real-world transportation network — its facilities, routes, shipments, and cargo — and enriches it with predictions, analytics, and optimization tools.

Instead of reacting to problems after they happen, a digital twin lets you **anticipate, simulate, and optimize** before real-world consequences occur.

## What Laplace does today

<Steps>
<Step title="Ingests your logistics data">
Laplace connects to your operational data sources (CTRCs, shipment records, facility information) and continuously syncs them into a unified system.
</Step>
<Step title="Visualizes your network">
An interactive map displays your logistics network — every facility, every route, every connection — updated in near-real-time. Click any unit to see its details, shipments, and risk profile.
</Step>
<Step title="Predicts cargo loss">
A prediction model scores every shipment (CTRC) at emission time with a loss probability. High-risk cargo is flagged so your team can act before losses occur.
</Step>
<Step title="Enables field verification">
The Collector module provides a mobile-optimized interface for field operators to verify high-risk cargo with photo evidence, closing the loop between prediction and action.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Platform modules

The Laplace Platform is organized into modules. Each organization has access to the specific modules included in their subscription.

| Module | Description | Status |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| **Digital Twin** | Interactive logistics network map with unit markers and route visualization | Available |
| **Digital Model** | Unit (facility) management with detailed views and operational data | Available |
| **Loss Prediction** | Cargo loss risk scoring with table view, collector, and dashboard | Available |
| **Database Browser** | Browse raw data tables (CTRCs, predictions, units, links) | Available |
| **Control Tower** | Centralized operations monitoring and alerting | Coming soon |
| **Load Consolidation** | Shipment consolidation optimization across the network | Coming soon |
